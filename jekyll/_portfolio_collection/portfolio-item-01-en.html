Description

The ROC Rooster App is designed for students and teachers of the ROC Friese Poort. The app ensures you always have your schedule at hand, even without internet.

In about four months, I went from an idea and design to realization, and from realization to implementation. I have published the first version of the app on the Google Play store on March 1, 2015. The app has currently between 500 and 1000 active users.
